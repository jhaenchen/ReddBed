ReddBed

Embeddable updating reddit posts for sharing and posting around the web.

For your website...

You can even make a Reddbed without coming to the site! Just enter the following code into your page html:

<div class="reddit_post"><a href="Put the target url here"></a></div><script src="https://rawgit.com/jhaenchen/ReddBed/master/src.js"></script>
